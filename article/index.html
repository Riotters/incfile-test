​Steps You Can Take Today to Get Your Home Business Off the Ground

February 25, 2020 in State Information

Now that you have a short list of names that are unique and not used by another business in your state, you can decide on your favorite. For each name on your list, go through the questions again and make sure the name fits with your intent for your business. Talk to the people you know, speak with potential customers and get feedback on the name. Then, you can make the final decision and get your business started.
